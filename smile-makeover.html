The Step-by-Step Process of Getting Veneers in Wong & Sim Dental

1
Consultation & Smile Design – Trial Smile
Your journey begins with a cosmetic consultation at Wong & Sim Dental. We’ll discuss your smile goals, assess your dental health, and design a personalized veneer plan. During this stage, a “trial smile” may be created to preview your new look.
2
Preparation & Temporary Veneers
To prepare your teeth, a minimal amount of enamel is gently removed. Temporary veneers are then placed to protect your teeth and help you get used to the look and feel of your new smile. These also serve as a preview, allowing us to gather feedback and make refinements before crafting your permanent veneers.
3
Veneer Delivery & Final Fit
Once your custom veneers are ready, they are securely bonded to your teeth using high-quality dental adhesive. Final adjustments are made to ensure a perfect fit, natural bite alignment, and flawless aesthetics. We’ll also conduct a detailed final check to ensure your veneers look stunning and feel completely comfortable.

Frequently Asked Veneer Questions

Are Veneers painful?
Local anaesthetic will be used during both the preparation and final application appointments to ensure you remain as comfortable as possible throughout the procedure and afterward.
Do Veneers look natural?
Yes! We customize each veneer to match your tooth shape, color, and size, ensuring a natural and aesthetic look.
Can anyone get Veneers?
Most people are good candidates, but those with severe enamel loss or gum disease may need other treatments first.
Are Veneers Difficult to Maintain?
Not at all—veneers are relatively easy to maintain. Consistent oral hygiene, avoiding habits that put excessive force on your teeth (like biting hard objects), and attending regular dental check-ups are all it takes to keep your veneers looking great and lasting for years.
How long do Veneers last?
With proper care and good oral hygiene, porcelain veneers can last 8-15 years or more.

Factors To Consider When Designing Your New Smile

1
Smile Symmetry
Ensuring your smile is balanced on both sides of your face for a harmonious look.
2
Shape of Teeth
Matching the tooth shape to your face shape for a cohesive appearance.
3
Size of Teeth
Adjusting the proportion of your teeth to enhance your smile’s aesthetics.
4
Gum Levels
Balancing the gum-to-teeth ratio for a pleasing, well-proportioned smile.
5
Angle of Teeth
Ensuring that the teeth align correctly to create an appealing visual flow.
6
Spaces Between Teeth
Managing gaps to prevent any distracting spaces that detract from your smile.
7
Reflection of Light
Enhancing the brightness and clarity of your teeth for a radiant smile.
8
‘Dark Corridors’
Minimizing the shadowy spaces at the corners of your mouth for a fuller look.
9
Lip Posture
Adjusting how much of your teeth show when you smile and at rest.
10
Translucency
Creating a natural translucency that makes your teeth look healthy and real.
